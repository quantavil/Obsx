## adjective
### **Updated Full Order of Adjectives:**

1. **Determiner** _(articles, possessives, demonstratives, etc.)_ тЖТ _a, an, the, my, his, their, this, those_
2. **Quantity (Quantitative Adjective)** тЖТ _few, many, several, some, all_
3. **Numeral (Cardinal & Ordinal Numbers)** тЖТ _one, two, three, first, second, third_
4. **Opinion** тЖТ _beautiful, ugly, interesting, boring_
5. **Size** тЖТ _big, small, tiny, huge_
6. **Age** тЖТ _old, young, new, ancient_
7. **Shape** тЖТ _round, square, oval, flat_
8. **Color** тЖТ _red, blue, dark, light_
9. **Proper Adjective (from Proper Nouns)** тЖТ _French, Indian, American, Shakespearean_
10. **Material** тЖТ _wooden, metal, plastic, cotton_
11. **Purpose** тЖТ _sleeping (as in "sleeping bag"), cooking (as in "cooking pot")_
12. **Noun** (the main noun being described)

### **Examples:**

- **My three beautiful large old round red Italian wooden dining** table. _(possessive + numeral + other adjectives)_
- **Those several big new square blue Japanese silk scarves.** _(demonstrative + quantitative + other adjectives)_
